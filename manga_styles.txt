Manga Style Guidelines for EKNMusic:

Background: #FFFFFF (white)
Sidebar: #F0F0F0 (light gray)
Text: #000000 (black)
Secondary text: #666666 or #333333
Borders: 3-4px solid #000000 (thick black borders)
Hover: #E8E8E8 or #F5F5F5
Active/Selected: #000000 background with #FFFFFF text
Buttons: Black background (#000000) with white text (#FFFFFF)
Or white background (#FFFFFF) with black border (3px solid #000000)

No gradients, no transparency, pure black and white with gray tones
